Connect 4 Rules (How to play the game)

  1. To be the first player to connect 4 of the same colored discs in a row
  2. First, decide who goes first and what color each player will have.
  3. Players must alternate turns, and only one disc can be dropped in each turn.
  4. On your turn, drop one of your colored discs from the top into any of the seven slots.
  5. The game ends when there is a 4-in-arow or a stalemate.
  6. The starter of the previous game goes second on the next game.
